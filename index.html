teachers

Apart from seeking knowledge of the Quran, students also look up to the Quran Teacher as a Role Model and the most Ideal Person in the society

It was narrated that Anas bin Malik said: "The Messenger of Allah ﷺ said: 'Allah has His own people among mankind.' They said: 'O Messenger of Allah ﷺ, who are they?' He said: 'The people of the Qur'an, the people of Allah and those who are closest to Him. '"(Sunan ibn Majah Hadith No. 215, Classed as Saheeh by Shaykh al-Albaani)

Therefore, at Academia Quran we ensure that the Teachers selected are of highest competency, skills and experience. Teachers are interviewed about their knowledge, places of acquiring knowledge and certifications, language and communication skills, previous experience in teaching Quran: Offline and Online.

Teachers also undergo an orientation and training Program to be aligned with our Academy’s mission and upgrade their skills as required.

In order to cater to students of varied Nationalities and Languages, we see to it that we have teachers from both Arab and non-Arab countries.

Being an online platform we are able to provide classes 24 hours a day, giving full liberty to students to choose their preferred time for classes whichever part of the world they may be from.

Separate Female teachers are assigned for Female students in order to maintain Hijaab. We train our teachers to understand the special care, attention and encouragement needed by young children to develop the love and interest with the Quran.
